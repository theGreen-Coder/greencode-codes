Privacy Policy#

Last Updated: 17-12-2025

Who we are#

Our website address is: https://thegreencode.org .

This website is purely informational. We do not offer user accounts, login functionality, or public comment sections.

What personal data we collect and why we collect it#

Comments and Media#

We do not allow comments or image uploads on our posts. Therefore, we do not collect personal data or metadata typically associated with these interactions.

Cookies and Analytics#

While we do not use cookies for user accounts (as there are no accounts), we use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our content.

Google Analytics uses cookies‚Äîsmall text files placed on your device‚Äîto collect standard internet log information and visitor behavior information in an anonymous form.

The information generated by the cookie about your use of the website includes:

  • Your IP address (which is anonymized).
  • Browser type and operating system.
  • Referring sites.
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site.

Why we collect this: We use this information to compile reports on website activity and to help us improve the content and user experience. We do not use this data to personally identify you.

Embedded content from other websites#

Articles on this site may include embedded content (e.g., YouTube videos, tweets, images). Embedded content from other websites behaves in the exact same way as if the visitor has visited the other website.

These websites may collect data about you, use cookies, embed additional third-party tracking, and monitor your interaction with that embedded content, including tracking your interaction with the embedded content if you have an account and are logged in to that website (e.g., if you are logged into Google while watching a YouTube video on our site).

Who we share your data with#

We do not sell, trade, or rent users’ personal identification information to others.

The only third party with whom data is shared is Google (for Analytics purposes). This data is processed to provide us with statistics on site usage.

How long we retain your data#

Since we do not have user profiles or comments, we do not hold personal user data on our own servers.

Data associated with cookies and advertising identifiers in Google Analytics is retained according to the standard Google Analytics retention period (typically 2 to 14 months, depending on settings), after which it is automatically deleted.

What rights you have over your data#

Since we do not hold personal accounts or comment records, we do not hold “exportable” personal files.

However, you can control your tracking preferences:

  • Cookies: You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer.
  • Google Analytics Opt-out: You can prevent Google Analytics from collecting data by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on .

Contact information#

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at: contactmegreencode@gmail.com
